## RECORDING NOTES

**Screen recordings needed:**
1. Landing page (hero section — "AI agents that run your trade business")
2. Dashboard — greeting header + 4 stat cards (Open Quotes 14, Active Jobs 9, Pending Requests 6, Revenue MTD $118,400)
3. Revenue chart — Feb 3 through Mar 5 trend line
4. AI Suggestions panel — hover/highlight each of the 4 suggestions
5. Jobs Pipeline — today's 6 jobs with tech assignments and times
6. Customer Requests — show Angela Rivera (High, 30 min ago) and Lisa Park (High, 1 hour ago)
7. Finance Overview — revenue/profit/cash grid + red overdue invoices section
8. Activity Feed — scroll through 7 entries showing agent actions
9. Quotes Pipeline — 4-stage funnel (Draft → Sent → Viewed → Accepted)
10. Back to landing page — waitlist form

**Tone:** Confident, direct, no fluff. Talk like you're explaining it to another contractor, not a tech audience. No jargon.

**Music:** Low-energy background beat. Nothing corporate. Think modern, clean, subtle.

**Pacing:** Fast cuts between screens. Don't linger. Every shot should be 3-5 seconds max except AI Suggestions and Activity Feed sections (let those breathe so viewers can read the data).

**Key numbers to make sure are visible on screen:**
- $118,400 revenue MTD (+14%)
- $38,600 in open quotes
- $12,800 Maria Santos repipe quote
- $3,400 Pacific Ridge HOA overdue (45 days)
- Invoice #1923 auto-generated
- 5 techs: Mike R., Tony M., Carlos S., Alex P., Jesse L.
