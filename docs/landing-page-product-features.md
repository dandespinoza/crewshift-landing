# CrewShift — Landing Page Product Features & Assets

## The Platform

CrewShift is the AI operations layer for trade contractors. It plugs into the tools you already use and runs your back office. Nine specialized agents handle the work that buries your business.

---

## The 9 Agents

### 1. Invoice Agent
Turns completed jobs into invoices automatically. Applies the right rates, markup, and tax. Sends to the right person in the right format. Tracks payment. Flags unbilled work. Handles GC pay applications, insurance billing, maintenance agreements, and membership programs. You stop chasing paperwork.

**Key outcomes:**
- Invoices go out same day, not 3-7 days later
- Unbilled revenue detected and flagged
- GC pay apps generated in AIA format with backup docs
- Lien waivers auto-generated with every invoice
- Real-time margin visibility per job

### 2. Estimate Agent
Builds professional proposals from job requests, photos, or plans. Prices work using your rate book. Generates Good/Better/Best options. Follows up until the customer decides. Converts approved estimates into contracts. Manages change orders through the life of the job.

**Key outcomes:**
- Estimates sent in minutes, not days
- Tiered options increase average ticket
- Follow-up sequences so nothing goes cold
- Win/loss tracking shows where you're leaving money
- Change orders documented and approved before work starts

### 3. Collections Agent
Takes over when invoices go unpaid. Runs outreach sequences tailored to customer type. Prepares you for every collection conversation with full context and talking points. Tracks lien deadlines. Packages accounts for attorney or agency when needed.

**Key outcomes:**
- No invoice falls through the cracks
- Lien rights preserved automatically
- Payment plans tracked and enforced
- AR aging shrinks, cash flow improves
- You know exactly who owes what and what to do about it

### 4. Finance Agent
Your AI controller. Real-time cash flow visibility, job costing, P&L by service line, expense tracking, payroll cost analysis, and tax prep. Reads from your accounting tool and gives you CFO-level insight in plain English.

**Key outcomes:**
- "Can I make payroll Friday?" answered instantly
- Know your true margin on every job
- Cash flow forecast 30/60/90 days out
- Tax documents organized for your accountant
- Expense anomalies caught early

### 5. Field-Ops Agent
The back-office side of dispatch. Decides who goes where based on skills, location, availability, and licenses. Manages capacity, prepares job briefings, coordinates schedule changes, and handles the logistics the owner or office manager would otherwise juggle manually.

**Key outcomes:**
- Right tech, right job, right time
- Schedule changes handled without chaos
- Overtime flagged before it happens
- Job briefings so techs arrive prepared
- Capacity planning so you know when to hire

### 6. Compliance Agent
Tracks everything that can shut you down, fine you, or lose you a job. Building violations, permits, inspections, insurance, licenses, OSHA, environmental, contracts, fleet, and government project requirements. Jurisdiction-aware starting with NYC.

**Key outcomes:**
- Never miss a deadline again
- Violation action plans generated automatically
- Permit forms pre-filled from your data
- License and insurance expiration tracked for every team member
- Weekly compliance digest with your full risk picture

### 7. Inventory Agent
Manages parts, materials, purchasing, and vendor pricing. Knows what's on each truck, what's in the warehouse, what needs reordering, and who has the best price. Prevents the two things that kill jobs: not having the part and overpaying for the part.

**Key outcomes:**
- Truck stock tracked and replenished
- Reorder points that actually work
- Vendor price comparison across suppliers
- Parts usage tied to jobs for true cost tracking
- "We don't have it" happens less

### 8. Customer Agent
Your AI CRM manager. Tracks customer relationships, automates review requests, manages follow-ups, identifies upsell opportunities, and flags at-risk accounts. Knows every interaction across every agent so nothing about a customer is lost.

**Key outcomes:**
- Review requests sent at the right time
- Repeat customers recognized and rewarded
- At-risk relationships flagged early
- Customer history available instantly
- Follow-ups that actually happen

### 9. Insights Agent
Cross-agent analytics and business intelligence. Pulls data from every other agent to show you how your business is actually performing. Revenue trends, margin analysis, team performance, customer patterns, and forecasting.

**Key outcomes:**
- One dashboard for the whole business
- Know which services make money and which don't
- Team performance visibility
- Seasonal trends and demand forecasting
- Data-driven decisions instead of gut feelings

## Platform Capabilities (Shared Across All Agents)

### AI Copilot
Talk to your business in plain English. "How much does ABC Properties owe me?" "What's my cash position?" "Schedule Mike for the boiler job tomorrow." "I got a violation." One conversation, every agent working behind it.

### Five-Tier Autonomy
You control how much the AI does on its own. Routine tasks run automatically. Sensitive actions wait for your approval. High-stakes situations escalate immediately with full context. The system learns your preferences and suggests when to give it more autonomy.

- **Auto** — runs without you (syncing data, calculations, tracking)
- **Communicate** — sends routine messages on your behalf (reminders, confirmations, receipts)
- **Review** — drafts it, you approve it (invoices, estimates, formal notices)
- **Prepare** — assembles everything you need for a conversation or meeting (talking points, documents, options)
- **Escalate** — alerts you immediately with full context (legal risk, financial exposure, safety)

### Plug Into Your Existing Tools
CrewShift connects to 100+ trade industry tools. QuickBooks, Xero, ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, Stripe, Gusto, Google Workspace, and more. No rip and replace. It works on top of what you already have.

### Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance
Starting with NYC (the hardest market), expanding to LA, Chicago, Philadelphia, and Miami-Dade. The system knows each city's codes, permit types, violation classes, deadlines, and department contacts.

### Agent Chaining
Agents don't work in silos. A completed job triggers the invoice agent. An unpaid invoice triggers collections. A violation triggers compliance, field-ops, and finance simultaneously. A new estimate triggers inventory to check stock and compliance to check permit requirements. Everything is connected.

### Communication Layer
Every agent can send emails, texts, in-app notifications, and push alerts to customers, techs, suppliers, GCs, and the owner. The agent decides what to say and when. The platform handles delivery, tracking, and read receipts.
